Liberstad City Fund / LCF

LCF is a for-profit organization that aims to support the development of Liberstad and the surrounding area.

Strategy Concept

Purpose

Help improve and accelerate the development of Norway's first private city, Liberstad, through strategic investments and partnerships. Investments into companies that operates with the ideas of freedom, non-aggression and self-organization.

Operational Rules

The LCF will always attempt to have a less than 50% share of any company. The LCF want to help with initial investment and growth investments, but not take a leading role in the running and organization of companies.

Operational Form

The LCF will operate similar to an ETF, Exchange Traded Fund. Issuance of stocks with happen on multiple different platforms and blockchains, excluding any government controlled platforms.

An exchange-traded fund (ETF) is a type of pooled investement security that holds multiple underlying assets, rather than only one. It allows you to invest into certain sectors or projects, without having to investigate and invest into each individual company.

The LCF will take part ownership in companies and hold other valuables including Bitcoin, crypto and precious metals.

The LCF will provide a quarterly report on holdings and pay dividends at quarterly basis.

Organizational Form

The board of the LCF consist of members that have been voted upon by the previous board. Adoption of a new board member requires 100% confidence vote from existing members.

Board membership require validation of holding an account with 100 000 CITY COIN for the entirety of the period of board membership.

The board is identified using decentralized identifiers (DID) that is part of the holding validation.

Proof of signing is provided on a regular basis when needed.

Profits

The board makes decision on payment of dividends each quarter. Assets and valuables that are not paids as dividends, are made available to Director for investment.

Director of Fund

The operations of the fund is controlled by the Director. The Director can be replaced by the board and requires 100% confidence vote from board members to be replaced. If the Director is part of the Board, their vote is not counted.

Become a shareholder

You become a shareholder by aquire one or more of the public offers listed above.

To become City Chain stocks shareholder, you need to aquire City Coin to perform the purchase. City Coin can be aquired using an atomic swap service that securely swaps from Bitcoin (and other) assets to City Coin (CITY). There are additional services and methods to aquire City Coin in Liberstad.

Blockcore Atomic Swaps: https://swap.blockcore.net/

Another option is to exchange any of your assets for XLM (on any major crypto exchange where lumens are quoted), and then exchange them for LCF tokens through permanent orders on Stellar (XLM->EURMTL->LCF) chain.
